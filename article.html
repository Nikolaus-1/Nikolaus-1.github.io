Ovid-Elsie Marching Band Allows Alumni to Showcase Their Talents

When is This Event Supposed to Take Place?

The Marching Band will be held at Ovid-Elsie High School on the field from 6:00 to 8:00 on Friday, Sep 20th. The band will be a mix of all grade levels, along with all graduates Who will be in attendance at this event?

What the 8th Graders/Alumni will Expect

This will be the first time Alumni will join the 8th graders for a performance. In preparation for this event, we questioned Stacy James about what she thought of the outcome of the performance, I believe the 8th grade will do fine during the performance, however, I am worried about whether we have enough Alumni for this event.

The Current Fear for The Band

The worry at this current time is the possibility that there may be too few Alumni for this performance to take place during the time from now till Sep 20th, 2024. This may contain a chance that the performance doesn't go as planned and is either postponed or canceled, because of the few Alumni in attendance.
